Anxious

ya albee entiii

Take it slow ya ro7albee 🤍 everything is gonna be alright, now take a deep breath, drink water and keep on saying this dua —

لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ إِنِّي كُنْتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ

This dua's story alone gives you hope 🌟 When Prophet Younis was inside the whale, he said this — and after that Allah said in the Quran:

﴿ وَذَا النُّونِ إِذْ ذَهَبَ مُغَاضِبًا فَظَنَّ أَن لَّن نَّقْدِرَ عَلَيْهِ فَنَادَىٰ فِي الظُّلُمَاتِ ﴾

Like — he was in the darkness, in the depths of the sea, inside a whale, and STILL Allah answered him. Inshallah Allah will remove all the anxiety in you ya albee 🤲

And I am here — I will never be far nor away. If you're anxious I will be there, your therapist and your calming medicine 💙 I will do my best to guide you through all the darkness until we see light and see you smile, shine bright, and your eyes radiating and shining brighter than the sun and stars.

My beautiful angel, ya ro7 el ro7 w noor al2lb 🌸
